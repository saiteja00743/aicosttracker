# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy for Credex AI

## Target Audience
Our primary target is **Series A to Pre-IPO Startups (50-1,000 employees)**.
- **Buyer Persona:** CFOs, VP of Finance, or Head of Engineering.
- **Pain Point:** They have no visibility into how many idle AI seats they are paying for across ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, and Copilot. They are overspending by 30%+ without realizing it because of auto-renewing credit cards from multiple team leads.

## The Viral Wedge (PLG Motion)
The **Free AI Spend Audit** acts as a powerful top-of-funnel wedge.
1. **Acquisition:** Founders see our landing page or a shared "redacted" report on Twitter/LinkedIn.
2. **Activation:** They input their stack in < 30 seconds.
3. **Value Delivery:** The Audit Engine instantly calculates the exact $ amount they are burning monthly. 
4. **The Hook:** If savings > $10k/yr, we offer a "Concierge Negotiation Service" (Sales-Led Motion) where we take a 10% cut of savings, or they can pay a flat $499/mo to use our infrastructure API gateway.

## Distribution Channels
1. **Product Hunt Launch:** Positioned as "The definitive AI tech-stack optimizer." High potential for "Product of the Day" due to the highly relevant nature of AI spending right now.
2. **Founder Networks:** Cold email outreach to founders who recently raised Series A/B funding (tracked via Crunchbase). Post-fundraising is when teams scale rapidly and tool sprawl begins.
3. **Viral Shareability:** Every generated report has a "Share with your team" button. When the CFO shares the report link in Slack, the entire leadership team sees the Credex brand.

## Competitive Advantage
Unlike traditional SaaS management tools (like Vendr or Ramp) that look at *all* software, we are hyper-specialized in AI pricing dynamics. We know that Cursor Pro ($20) is identical to Cursor Business ($40) except for privacy toggles. This domain expertise allows us to provide highly specific, actionable advice rather than generic "you have 3 project management tools."
